Project Details

In the application user will be asked to remember a specific moment that reminds them by the provided words and to add as many details as possible. The system gives the feedback in few seconds, after providing a specific response user will be then asked to click on the tab marked ‘details’ to provide some extra description about the memory. After user have given answer to each word our program will give you feedback on whether the memory is specific enough. If the answer is not specific enough, then we will ask you some extra follow-up questions to help you remember more details. After the 9 exercises using cue-words, there will be another 2 similar exercises. For these exercises you will not be asked for a memory in response to a cue word. Instead we will ask you about what happened at a particular time in your life in the recent past. We will explain more about these exercises later on. After the exercises are finished we will ask you five short questions to evaluate the session.
